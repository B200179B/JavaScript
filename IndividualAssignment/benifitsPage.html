Cake can help beat depression


John Whaite, the hugely popular winner of the GBBO Series 3, spoke out at 2013 year about how baking has helped him battle depression. He’s not alone; experts agree that baking helps give people struggling with mental illness a focus, a structure, and a basic sense of achievement.



Cake Can Provide energy

Carbohydrates are one of the major sources of energy in plenty of foods. And cakes & cupcakes are generally made by mixing flour and sugar together, which are excellent sources of carbohydrates. They can provide the entire body, including muscles, brain and nervous system, with a sufficient amount of energy. In addition to that, the fats present in cakes are also good sources of energy.



Cake Can Stops Tantrums

One minute you’ve got a crazed little person screaming like their brain is on fire, the next, you wheel out some cake and the birds are tweeting love songs. Therefore not only is cake simply amazing, but also vital to your sanity as a mum.
